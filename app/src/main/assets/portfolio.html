About O-Invest

O-Invest is a modern investment management application built using Kotlin, designed to empower investors to manage and grow their portfolios with ease. With real-time tracking, secure transactions, and a minimalist design, O-Invest makes smart investing accessible to all.

Key Features

Portfolio Tracking

Monitor investments and returns in real-time with detailed analytics.

Investment Recommendations

Personalized suggestions tailored to your risk profile and goals.

Secure Transactions

Industry-standard encryption ensures your financial activities are protected.

Modern UI/UX

Enjoy a clean, intuitive, and seamless user experience crafted for investors.

Technology Stack

Language: Kotlin (Native Android App)

Database: Local Persistence with Cloud Sync Option

Security: Encrypted Transactions

Design: Minimalist and Modern

Target Audience

O-Invest is ideal for individual investors, financial advisors, and young professionals looking for a reliable and easy-to-use investment management platform.

Future Plans

  • Stock exchange and crypto market integrations
  • AI-powered financial advising
  • iOS and Web platform versions
